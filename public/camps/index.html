Generations Summer Camps

Updated for 2017

Below, please find our list of summer camp offerings. Toddler Enrichment Camps are open to children 18 months – 3 years of age. All other camps are open to children 3 – 12 years of age.

For all offerings, a balanced snack will be provided each day. Students enrolled in Cooking with Kids will be making their lunch each day. For all other options, we ask that students bring a nut-free lunch each day. (Note: Students participating in the Toddler Enrichment Camps will not have lunch at camp.)

Space is limited. Please email Rita Pace at rita_pace@comcast.net to reserve your spot today!

June 2017

June 19 – 23, 2017 Toddler Fun with Art & Music
 9:00AM-Noon$165
 Instructor: Rita Pace

Throughout the week, students will have an opportunity to explore music and art. Children will sing, dance, and experiment with different art mediums and musical instruments.

June 19 – 23, 2017 Fun with Art & Music
 9:00AM-1:00PM$165
 Instructor: Christine Pace

Students will explore a variety of arts including, but not limited to, hand arts, visual arts, and the preforming arts. Students will participate in music each day and have the opportunity to try several different instruments.

July 2017

July 10 – 14, 2017 Supper Camp
 9:00AM-3:00PM$315
 Instructors: Rita and Christine Pace

The children will work to create a balanced, nutritional meal for you and your family. Every day, each child will leave with a complete meal from main dish to dessert.

July 17 – 21, 2017 Toddler Cooking Basics
 9:00AM-Noon$200
 Instructor: Rita Pace

Children will work together to create simple recipes with a focus around good table manners, proper place settings, and healthy eating habits.

July 17 – 21, 2017 Cooking with Kids
 9:00AM-1:00PM$200
 Instructor: Christina Pace

Children will work together to plan and create balanced snacks and meals for the week. Each meal will include a main course, side, and dessert. The week will end with a picnic for the entire family.

August 2017

August 7 – 11, 2017 Toddler Science Exploration
 9:00AM-Noon$165
 Instructor: Rita Pace

Students will have the opportunity to explore science through simple experimentation involving water, color, and basic household supplies.

August 7 – 11, 2017 Full STEAM Ahead
 9:00AM-1:00PM$165
 Instructor: Christina Pace

Students will work with instructors to begin to develop hypotheses, conduct experiments, and make conclusions based on simple data. Students will be encouraged to work together and think creatively when presented with design problems.

August 14 – 18, 2017 Cooking with Kids
 9:00AM-1:00PM$200
 Instructors: Rita and Christina Pace

Children will work together to plan and create balanced snacks and meals for the week. Each meal will include a main course, side, and dessert. The week will end with a picnic for the entire family.
