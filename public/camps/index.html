Generations Summer Camps

Below, please find our list of summer camp offerings. Unless otherwise stated, all camps are open to children 3-12 years of age. A balanced snack will be provided each day. Students enrolled in Cooking with Kids will be making their lunch each day. For all other options, we ask that students bring a nut-free lunch each day.
